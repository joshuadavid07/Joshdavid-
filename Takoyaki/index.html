We Are Tako

At Tako, we bring the authentic taste of Japanese street food to your neighborhood. Our master chefs have perfected the art of making takoyaki - those delicious golden balls that are crispy on the outside and irresistibly creamy on the inside. Using only the freshest ingredients and traditional cooking techniques, we create takoyaki that transport you straight to the bustling streets of Osaka. Whether you're a takoyaki traditionalist or adventurous foodie, our diverse menu has something special for everyone.
